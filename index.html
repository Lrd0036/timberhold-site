Timberhold

A cozy isometric lumber RPG. Fell trees, mill logs, build out your camp, and push the frontier outward — one shipment at a time.

What you'll do

Harvest

Walk up to a tree and hold to fell, strip the limbs, and buck the trunk into stacked logs.

Mill

Drag logs to the sawmill to turn raw timber into planks that sell for more.

Sell & build

Drop logs at the merchant. Spend coins on camp structures, axes, and hauling upgrades.

Push the frontier

Each milestone unlocks a new biome ring with rarer timber species and tougher trees.

Workbench crafts

Build the workbench to craft sharper saw blades, sign posts, and rope coils.

Day to night

A short day/night cycle shifts the light, mood, and ambient soundscape.

Built with respect for your time

  • No accounts, no logins.
  • No ads, no tracking, no analytics.
  • Saves stay on your device.
  • Playable fully offline.
  • Designed for one-handed play with a virtual thumbstick and a single hold-to-act button.
